The Manipal Society

WHAT WE DO

/

We are Manipal's First Charity Management Blog which aims to bridge that gap between the money you donate and the impact it has for the extreme poor,orphanages, oldage homes making your donation more personal and immediate by helping you to give as directly to a specific recipient as possible.
The blog will be full of touching personal stories, fascinating insights into the process and interesting examinations of how we “do” charity today, discussing the efforts of various home clubs like YouthForVolunteering and VSO to help make lives of these people better. It is platform for fundraising dealing with lots of causes daily,The Manipal Society is a wealth of information and inspiration. For anyone considering launching a fundraising campaign,posts are full of ideas and tip to help you maximise the amount you raise. For everyone else, the blog is full of great stories and initiatives worth knowing about.
